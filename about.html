About

Welcome! Thank you for stopping by.
My name is Eve, and I am a college student from the greater New York City area. This blog was born out of my quarantine baking hobby. You may notice some of the content is of Armenian origin! My family immigrated from Armenia, and I hope to also use this platform to showcase our traditional recipes that I continue to make with my mom. You will also find recipes I have randomly tried, modified, and gotten great feedback on from family members.
