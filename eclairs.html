Easy Eclairs

Effort: Easy/Medium

30 mins (Prep), 40 mins (Bake)

Ingredients

Pâte à Choux Pastry

  • 1 stick butter (8 tablespoons)
  • 6 eggs
  • 200 mL (0.85 cup) white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 tablespoons cow's milk
  • 2 ⅓ cups pastry flour

Custard

  • 2 eggs
  • 100 mL (~0.4 cup) white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons pastry flour
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons butter

Chocolate glaze

  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 100 mL (~0.4 cup) white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cow's milk
  • 2-3 tablespoons of butter

Preparation

Pâte à Choux Pastry

  1. Take the butter out of the fridge for a few hours. It has to be soft (room temperature).

Custard

  1. Take the butter out of the fridge for a few hours. It has to be soft (room temperature).

Putting it all together

  1. Slice eclairs midway (just enough to open them like a clam). Use tablespoon to stuff in custard.
  2. (Optional) Use a sifter to coat eclair tops with powdered sugar.
