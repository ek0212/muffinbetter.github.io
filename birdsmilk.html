Bird's Milk Cake

Recipe

Pastry

  • 3 eggs
  • 200 mL (0.85 cup) white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Cream

  • ⅓ cup melted coconut oil or extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ cup honey or maple syrup
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Preparation Notes

  • Combine wet and dry (without blueberries) ingredients in mixer (use paddle attachment for KitchenAid stand mixer).
  • Mix in blueberries briefly on low speed with mixer.
  • Bake at 400 degrees Farenheit for 19 minutes.
